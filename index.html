Executive Summary: The Path to Profitability

This RTD tequila business model demonstrates a scalable path to profitability driven by manufacturing efficiency and volume growth. The core value creation mechanism is simple: as production volume increases, unit costs decline dramatically, allowing margins to expand from 20.6% to 50.8% over three years.

üéØ The Critical Insight: We don't need to raise prices to improve margins. We achieve margin expansion purely through operational efficiency and scale economics. Every volume milestone we hit automatically improves our unit economics.

Why These Numbers Matter: The difference between initial production (11,700 units/month) and scaled production (68,400 units/month) isn't just about revenue growth‚Äîit's about fundamentally transforming the business economics. At scale, our per-case COGS drops 35%, converting what would be a 20% margin business into a 50% margin business with the same pricing.

Year 1 Focus: The first year is about proving product-market fit while managing cash burn. We project $861K in EBITDA losses, which translates to roughly $72K monthly burn. With a $1M seed round, we have ~14 months of runway to hit our first major milestone: 100K+ units to unlock mass production pricing.

The Inflection Point: Break-even becomes achievable around 1M cases annually (83K cases/month), which we project to reach in Year 4-5. At this volume, gross profit per case ($8.61) finally exceeds operating cost per case ($9.06), and continued growth drives profitability.
